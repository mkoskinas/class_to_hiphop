Hi, this is a classical-to-hip-hop track Recommender

You can start getting hip hop playlist recommendations by adding your classical piece's trackId from Spotify in the following URL:

http://35.198.186.156/api/recommend?track_id='your_classical_track_Id_here'&popularity_threshold=0.9&playlist_len=10

popularity_threshold: how popular you want your results to be (0 to 1)
playlist_len: the no. of the returned tracks
